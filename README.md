**CEO & Founder of [OffDeck](https://offdeck.com)** — a growth studio for venture-backed startups and public companies.

### Beyond work

I was born with a cleft lip and wrote a children's book about it in kindergarten called _My Puzzling Smile_. It's now used by doctors, hospitals, and schools worldwide — funded by Procter & Gamble and recognized with a Walt Disney "Dreamers & Doers" award. The book and its companion are still available through [Clefts United](https://cleftsunited.com), which I founded in 2008.
